Welcome to Nagpur

Nagpur, officially the Nagpur District, is a city and a union territory in the central Indian state of Maharashtra, bordered by Mumbai to the north, Pune to the east, and Goa to the south. With a population of 22 million as of 2021, Nagpur is the 15th most populous city in India, and the 24th most populous district. It is the capital of the Nagpur Division, and is the most populous city in the Maharashtra state. Nagpur is a major commercial, cultural, and educational hub, with numerous attractions and events throughout the year.

Nagpur, located in central India, is often referred to as the "Orange City" due to its extensive production of oranges. It's a major commercial and political hub, known for its rich history and cultural diversity. The city boasts landmarks like the famous Deekshabhoomi, a significant Buddhist site, and the spacious Futala Lake, popular for its scenic beauty. Nagpur also serves as an important transportation hub, with its central location connecting various regions. With a blend of modern infrastructure and traditional charm, Nagpur is a vibrant city that reflects both the past and the present of India.
